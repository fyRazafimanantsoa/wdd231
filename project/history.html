My Coding Journey

The Beginning

I started this page on July 25th as a way to share my path in learning code and Frontend development. At that time, I had just begun exploring HTML and CSS—about a month and a half into my journey. The excitement was palpable, even as I encountered the first challenges.

Overcoming Challenges

Like many beginners, I faced hurdles: understanding responsive design, debugging code, and getting comfortable with JavaScript fundamentals. Each mistake was a lesson, and every solution built my confidence to press forward.

Milestones & Successes

Every small victory—from creating my first interactive webpage to designing a layout I was proud of—became a milestone. I learned that persistence and a willingness to experiment are key in mastering coding.

Looking Ahead

Today, my journey continues as I explore new technologies and deepen my understanding of code. I hope my story inspires you to start your own adventure—remember, every expert was once a beginner!
